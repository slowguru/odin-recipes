Pico De Gallo (Salsa Fresca)

Classic & Clean: Classic Mexican Salsa

Pico de gallo is a fresh, uncooked Mexican salsa made from a simple mix of chopped tomatoes, onions, cilantro, jalapeños or serrano peppers, lime juice, and salt. Unlike blended salsas, Pico de gallo is chunky and vibrant, offering a crisp texture and a refreshing burst of flavor with every bite. Adding brightness and acidity to dishes, making it perfect for tacos, burritos, grilled meats, eggs, nachos, and more. Its flavor is clean and zesty, with just the right amount of heat and tang, making it a go-to condiment in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine. It’s incredibly versatile, and variations can include ingredients like pineapple, mango, cucumber, or corn for a creative twist. Simple, colorful, and always fresh, pico de gallo is a staple that enhances almost any savory dish.

  • Serves: 4-6 people as a topping or sauce
  • Prep Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe Roma tomatoes, diced
  • ½ medium white onion, finely chopped
  • 1–2 jalapeños, finely chopped (seeds removed for less heat)
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime (about 2 tablespoons)
  • ½ teaspoon salt (or to taste)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the diced tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, and cilantro.
  2. Add lime juice and salt. Stir to combine well.
  3. Add more salt or lime juice if needed. For extra heat, leave some jalapeño seeds in.
  4. Rest at room temp for 10–15 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld. Or refrigerate if making ahead.

Tips

  • Use ripe but firm tomatoes to avoid a watery salsa,removing seeds and excess pulp to avoid a soggy salsa.
  • For extra brightness, add a bit of lime zest.
  • To mellow the onion, soak chopped onion in cold water for 5 minutes before mixing.
  • Lime zest adds an extra citrus punch if desired.

Freshly made pico de gallo is a celebration of simplicity. The first spoonful bursts with flavor—the juiciness of ripe tomatoes, the bite of crisp onion, the spark of lime, and the unmistakable zing of fresh cilantro. Every element is raw, bright, and alive, layered with just the right touch of salt and heat from the chopped chile. It’s not just a topping—it’s a burst of freshness that elevates the entire dish. No cooking required, yet it tastes like it took more effort than it did. Light, colorful, and endlessly versatile, pico de gallo brings life to the plate in every bite.
